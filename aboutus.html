Our Story

It started with a simple problem: finding reliable notes the night before an exam was impossible. We realized that while information is everywhere, organized information is rare.

BTechBook.in was built to fix this. We curate the best resources from toppers, professors, and standard textbooks to create a one-stop repository for students.
